--- layout: default title: Home --- Astrophysics program simulating the evolution of star systems based on the fast multipole method on adaptive Octrees.
Multi-physics
Applications
Parallelism Octotiger used the asynchronous many-task system HPX for scheduling of light-weighed tasks. HPX is the C++ standard library for parallelism and concurrency. The compute kernels are written in Kokkos to provide support for NVIDIA and AMD GPUs.
